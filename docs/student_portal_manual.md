Welcome to the Learning Systems College (LSC) Student Registration Management System (SRMS). This manual will guide you through the registration process, portal access, and available features.

---

## First-Time Registration Process

### Step 1: Initial Application
1. Navigate to the LSC website and locate the "Apply Now" button
2. Complete the online application form with your personal details
3. Upload required documents:
   - Academic transcripts
   - Identification documents
   - Passport photograph
   - Payment receipt (if applicable)

### Step 2: Payment Process
1. Select your payment method:
   - Bank transfer
   - Online payment gateway
   - Mobile money
2. Complete the payment for application fees
3. Upload payment proof/receipt

### Step 3: Wait for Administrative Review
1. Your application will be reviewed by the admissions team
2. You will receive an email notification when your application is approved
3. The email will contain instructions for next steps

### Step 4: First-Time Login Setup
1. After approval, you will receive an email with login instructions
2. Visit the portal at `http://localhost/srms/first_time_login.php`
3. Enter the email address used for your application
4. You will receive a temporary password via email
5. On first login, you will be required to:
   - Set a new password
   - Verify your contact information
   - Complete your profile

### Step 5: Complete Registration
1. Once logged in, navigate to the "Complete Registration" section
2. Select your desired programme and intake
3. Register for required courses
4. Submit your registration for administrative approval

## Returning Student Access

### Logging In
1. Visit the main portal: `http://localhost/srms/login.php`
2. Enter your student number as your username
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login"

### Forgotten Password
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address or student number
3. Follow the instructions sent to your email
4. Reset your password

### Account Recovery
If you're unable to access your account:
1. Contact the IT support desk
2. Provide your student number and personal details
3. Verification will be required before account recovery

## Portal Features Guide

### Dashboard
- **Overview**: Your main landing page showing important notifications, upcoming deadlines, and quick access to common features
- **Quick Links**: Direct access to registration, course management, and grades
- **Notifications**: Alerts about registration periods, grade updates, and announcements

### Registration Section
#### For New Intakes:
1. Navigate to "Course Registration"
2. Select your programme and intake
3. Browse available courses
4. Select required and elective courses
5. Submit registration for approval
6. Monitor approval status

#### For Returning Students:
1. Go to "Course Registration"
2. Select your current term/semester
3. Add/drop courses if within the deadline
4. Submit changes for approval

### Programme Management
- **View Programme Details**: Access information about your programme curriculum
- **Course Progress**: Track completed and pending courses
- **Academic Timeline**: View your academic schedule and milestones

- **Enrolled Courses**: See all courses you're currently enrolled in
- **Course Materials**: Access lecture notes, assignments, and resources
- **Instructor Information**: Contact details and office hours for course instructors

### Grades and Results
- **Current Grades**: View ongoing assessment scores
- **Historical Results**: Access past semester results
- **Grade Reports**: Download official grade reports

### Financial Management
- **Fee Statement**: View your current financial obligations
- **Payment History**: Track all payments made

- **Financial Aid**: Apply for scholarships or financial assistance

### Personal Profile
- **Update Information**: Edit personal details, contact information, and emergency contacts

### Communication Tools
- **Announcements**: Stay updated with college announcements
- **Messaging**: Communicate with instructors and administrators

### Documents and Downloads
- **Admission Letters**: Download your admission letter
- **Course Materials**: Access textbooks, reading materials, and resources
- **Certificates**: Download completion certificates (when available)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Problems
- **Issue**: Cannot login with correct credentials
- **Solution**: 
  1. Clear browser cache and cookies
  2. Try a different browser
  3. Ensure Caps Lock is off
  4. Reset your password if necessary

#### Registration Issues
- **Issue**: Cannot register for courses
- **Solution**:
  1. Check registration period - ensure you're registering within the allowed timeframe
  2. Verify programme prerequisites are met
  3. Check for any holds on your account
  4. Contact academic advisor if issues persist

#### Technical Problems
- **Issue**: Portal is slow or not responding
- **Solution**:
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Close other browser tabs/applications
  3. Try accessing during off-peak hours
  4. Contact IT support if problems continue

#### Mobile Access
- The portal is responsive and works on mobile devices
- For best experience, use the latest version of Chrome, Safari, or Firefox
- Consider using your phone's browser rather than opening links in social media apps

### Browser Requirements
- **Supported Browsers**: Chrome (latest), Firefox (latest), Safari (latest), Edge (latest)
- **Unsupported**: Internet Explorer, older browser versions
- **Recommended Screen Resolution**: 1024x768 or higher

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

**Q: What if I don't receive the confirmation email?**
A: Check your spam/junk folder. If not found, contact technical support with your application details.

**Q: Can I register for courses outside my programme?**
A: This depends on prerequisites and availability. Consult with your academic advisor.

**Q: How do I know if my registration is approved?**
A: Check the "Registration Status" section on your dashboard or the course registration page.

**Q: What should I do if I encounter technical issues?**
A: Try clearing your browser cache first. If issues persist, contact technical support with detailed information about the problem.
